"ITS NOT JUST THE COFFEE"

Small Beginnings

Founded in 2018. Komu Lale is a retail coffee startup that wants to serve high quality coffee to its customers. The name Komu Lale is taken from the word forest which is our hope to grow fast, strong, tall & support everything around us. We want our existence to set a new standard of quality coffee for consumers.

Utilizing our network and experience, we use the latest technology and the best coffee blends. Coffee from selected farmers, high quality beans are processed and roasted to perfection and passed on by skilled baristas. Who are always eager to prepare a special cup of happiness for you.

Our Vision

We envision that our customers was able to enjoy not only our coffee, but also the hospitality that was provided in Komu Lale. We are proud of our coffee and we encourage you to share it with friends. We blend quality, health, humanity, beauty and flavour, bringing happiness through sharing. Each day we work towards our mission of quality and sharing by offering a fresh taste of Komu Lale’s coffee.
