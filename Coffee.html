OUR COFFEE



COMMITMENT IN IDEALISM

In line with our campaign: "Yes, I Drink Indonesian Coffee", Komu Lale is committed to using ONLY the best Indonesian coffee beans. After all, Indonesian coffee is not inferior to coffee from abroad.

We use two variants of coffee: Arabica and Robusta. For Arabica we use "Specialty Arabica" grade, while for Robusta we use "Fine Robusta" grade. The best quality in its class.

We currently provide 8 best origins from various plantations spread across Indonesia, one of which is from our own plantation on the slopes of Mount Arjuno, East Java. As time goes by, we plan to add more coffee origin variants so that Indonesians can enjoy the best coffee beans from all over Indonesia at affordable prices and with ease.





QUALITY

We understand that the quality of the aroma and flavor of coffee beans is of utmost importance. We at Komu Lale realize this, therefore we ensure that every step of the coffee bean journey, from picking to brewing plays an important role in determining the quality of your one cup of coffee.
